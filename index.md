# Étienne Tajeuna

Professor and researcher in computer science, with interests in graph representation learning, co-evolving time series, survival analysis, and context-aware artificial intelligence.

My research focuses on designing machine learning methods for complex evolving systems, with applications in social networks, cybersecurity, finance, health, and sports analytics.

## Research highlights

- Graph representation learning for dynamic and complex systems
- Modeling co-evolving time series through contextual and structural learning
- Survival analysis and event prediction
- Explainable and context-aware AI
- Applications to fraud detection, cybersecurity, and sports outcome prediction

## Current affiliations

- Professor of Computer Science, Université du Québec en Outaouais
- Founder, MoDCAI
